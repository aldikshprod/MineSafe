What is MineSafe?

MineSafe is a smart bracelet designed to protect miners and oil and gas industry workers in hazardous working conditions. Our device combines advanced gas detection technology, GPS tracking, and emergency communication.

The project idea emerged after analyzing accident statistics in the extractive industry. We recognized the need to create a personal wearable device that could prevent dangerous situations and save lives.

MineSafe is designed for harsh operating conditions: it is waterproof, shock-resistant, and has a long battery life.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about MineSafe

What warranty is provided for MineSafe bracelets?

All MineSafe bracelet models come with a 24-month warranty from the date of purchase. The warranty covers all manufacturing defects and malfunctions not related to mechanical damage of the device. For large corporate clients, warranty extension up to 36 months is possible.

How often do the sensors need calibration?

The recommended frequency for sensor calibration is once every 6 months to ensure maximum measurement accuracy. Our company provides sensor calibration services, as well as training for client specialists to perform this procedure independently.

Is integration with existing safety systems possible?

Yes, MineSafe is designed to integrate with most popular safety and monitoring systems. We provide APIs for integration with your existing SCADA, MES, and other corporate solutions. Custom integration options are also available.

How is device maintenance performed?

MineSafe device maintenance includes regular charging (depending on the model), periodic sensor calibration, and software updates. We offer comprehensive service contracts that include regular technical maintenance, replacement of malfunctioning devices, and consultative support.

Does the device work in areas without cellular coverage?

Yes, MineSafe bracelets continue to function even in areas without cellular coverage. In such cases, the device stores data locally and transmits it when a connection becomes available. For large facilities, we offer solutions using local mesh networks or with the installation of special repeaters to ensure communication in underground conditions.
